ready4 adopts a collaborative approach to developing a re-usable and updatable model of the systems that shape the mental health and wellbeing of young people.

{{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="light">}} {{< cardpane >}} {{< card header="People" >}} Modelling the characteristics, relationships, behaviours, risk factors and outcomes of young people and those important to them. {{< /card >}} {{< card header="Places" >}} Modelling demographic, environmental and proximity drivers of access, equity and outcomes. {{< /card >}} {{< /cardpane >}} {{< cardpane >}} {{< card header="Platforms" >}} Modelling the optimal staffing and configuration of support services for young people. {{< /card >}} {{< card header="Programs" >}} Modelling the affordability, value for money and appropriate targeting of interventions for young people. {{< /card >}} {{< /cardpane >}} {{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/lead >}}

ready4 is being developed as multiple independent computational modelling projects which all use a common framework to iterate over three steps.

{{< /blocks/lead >}} {{< blocks/section color="dark">}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-connectdevelop" title="Develop Model" %}} Abstract and generalised representations of systems are written in computer code. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-database" title="Add Data" %}} Model is tailored to a specific decision context and set of assumptions by adding relevant data. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-chart-line" title="Run Analyses" %}} Model is applied to data to generate insight. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/lead>}}
